Beans are unique food

Beans are the mature forms of legumes. They are also considered a vegetable. Green peas, Green lima beans and string beans are not considered part of the beans subgroup.

The most common types of beans are:

  • Black Beans
  • Black-Eyed Peas
  • Cannellini Beans
  • Chickpeas
  • Great Northern Beans
  • Kidney Beans
  • Pinto Beans
  • Fava Beans

For detail on varieties of beans you may go to Beans Classifications.
